Action Plan

  • Target Audience: General public, environmentalists, policymakers, students, and anyone interested in water issues.

Filming:

  • Conduct interviews with experts and individuals affected by water pollution.
  • Capture more cinematic footage of polluted water bodies and restoration projects in action.
  • Gather B-roll footage and sound effects to enhance the film's narrative.
  • Ensure high-quality audio and video recording.

Post-Production:

  • Assemble the footage into a cohesive and engaging narrative.
  • Add graphics, animations, and music to enhance the film's message.
  • Refine the script and voiceover narration.
  • Have backup plans for filming locations, interviews, and equipment in case of unforeseen circumstances.

Timeline:

  • Track progress regularly and make adjustments as needed.
