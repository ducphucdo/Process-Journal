Experimenting

I did some test shots with different subjects and environments to determine the right camera setting, exposure, and movement for various footage.
You may have notice that the colour in these 3 footages are very flat and desaturated, that is because I used a Log picture profile in my camera. Using a Log format helps to increase the dynamic range of the camera and having more flexible colour grading ability later in post-production.
